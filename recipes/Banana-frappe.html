Banana-frappe

Banana-frappe is a delicious and refreshing drink made from only two ingredints:

    milk
    banana

To make a banana-frappe, follow these steps:

    Peel the banana and divide into as many pieces as possible
    Put the banana pieces into a blender
    Pour approximately 200ml of milk into the blender

    Blend the mixture until smooth
    Pour into a glass and enjoy!
